About Me

I am a Master's student studying AI with a specialization in Cognitive Science at Vrije Universiteit in Amsterdam. Before moving to Amsterdam I studied Computer Science at the Colorado School of Mines, where I also minored in Public Affairs as part of the McBride Honors Program.

In my free time I like to make music and art and have an ongoing art project called Perrywinkle. At School of Mines I was a music/visual arts editor for High Grade, where some of my artistic work has been published. I also enjoy staying active, while at School of Mines I was the president of the running club. Additionally, I am often traveling and learning different languages. In 2022 I studied abroad and conducted an independent study of Spanish Language and Culture in Oviedo, Spain. There I met many friends I have continued to visit while living in Europe.

Apart from being a full-time student I am a scientific programmer for the Social AI Lab at Vrije Universiteit where I am the primary developer of the Social Interaction Cloud (SIC), the Python framework used for courses and robotics experiments. You can find my CV and read more about my past projects all on this website.
